# User Stories: Truck Parts Management System

## Epic 1: Catalog & Inventory Management
- **US-1.1**: As a Warehouse Operator, I want to view a search-friendly catalog of all truck parts, so that I can check stock status, SKU, and OEM numbers quickly.
- **US-1.2**: As a Warehouse Manager, I want to add new parts or edit details of existing ones, so that the catalog remains accurate.
- **US-1.3**: As a Warehouse Operator, I want to restock items easily when new shipments arrive.

## Epic 2: Point of Sale (POS) & Checkout
- **US-2.1**: As an Operator, I want to add multiple parts to a transaction cart, validating that the order quantity does not exceed current stock levels.
- **US-2.2**: As an Operator, I want to input customer info and custom discount levels for a transaction.
- **US-2.3**: As a Customer/Operator, I want to download/print a professional PDF sales invoice immediately upon completing a transaction.

## Epic 3: Sales Tracking & Analytics
- **US-3.1**: As an Admin, I want to see visual summaries of top-selling items and inventory layouts.
- **US-3.2**: As an Admin, I want to view a full history ledger of past invoices and have the option to re-download duplicate PDF copies.
